Brian | Creative & Content Producer

I'm a Creative Business student with a passion for bringing stories to life through digital media.

Ever since I was young, I've been fascinated by the world of content from the narrative pull of video games to the energy of a live stream. This has led me to explore every facet of production, whether directing behind the camera or engaging an audience in front of it. For me, the thrill is in creating something memorable that people can watch, hear, and connect with.

I believe in creating my own opportunities. This philosophy led me to co-found InCulture, a podcast I grew over two years that lead up to an independently produced 12-hour non-stop livestream. Building on that success, I launched Cre8ive, transforming a three-person show into a full live production with a highly engaged community.

Beyond Production: Writing & Connecting

My creative drive extends to the written word; in my personal time, I'm a writer and poet. This dual passion for creating and producing has been my key to building a strong, diverse network. I've had the pleasure of connecting with high-potential emerging artists in the music and art scenes, as well as with my talented peers in content creation.

It's this hands-on, entrepreneurial spirit that I bring to every project. I thrive on building communities and bringing ambitious ideas to life.
